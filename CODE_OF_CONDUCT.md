# 🌟 Code of Conduct  
### For the Stays Project

The Stays community is built on respect, curiosity, and collaboration. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for everyone participating in the project — contributors, maintainers, product owners, and community members.

Our goal is to foster a safe, inclusive, and productive environment where people of all backgrounds and skill levels can learn, build, and grow together.

---

## 💛 Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Stays project a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age  
- Body size  
- Disability  
- Ethnicity  
- Gender identity or expression  
- Level of experience  
- Nationality  
- Personal appearance  
- Race  
- Religion (or lack thereof)  
- Sexual identity or orientation  
- Socioeconomic status  

We are committed to creating a space where everyone feels welcome and valued.

---

## 🤝 Our Standards

### **Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:**

- Showing empathy and kindness  
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints  
- Offering constructive feedback  
- Taking responsibility for mistakes  
- Focusing on what’s best for the community  
- Celebrating the contributions of others  

### **Examples of unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination  
- Insults, derogatory comments, or personal attacks  
- Public or private harassment  
- Publishing others’ private information without permission  
- Disruptive or intentionally harmful behavior  
- Dismissing or belittling others’ work or experience  

---

## 🧑‍⚖️ Responsibilities of Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct  
- Reviewing and addressing issues respectfully and consistently  
- Taking appropriate action when unacceptable behavior occurs  
- Ensuring fair treatment regardless of contributor identity  

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

## 🚫 Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.

Reports will be handled with confidentiality to the fullest extent possible.

---

## 🧭 Enforcement Guidelines

When a violation occurs, maintainers may take actions such as:

### **1. Correction**  
A private, gentle reminder about the Code of Conduct.

### **2. Warning**  
A clear, written warning explaining the violation and expected behavior.

### **3. Temporary Ban**  
A temporary removal from participation in project spaces.

### **4. Permanent Ban**  
A permanent removal from the project community.

The severity of the action depends on the nature and frequency of the violation.

---

## 🌍 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub, discussions, issues, PRs)  
- Community events or meetings  
- Any public or private communication related to the project
