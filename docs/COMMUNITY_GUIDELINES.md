# 🌍 Community Guidelines  
### How we collaborate, communicate, and grow together

The Stays community is built on kindness, curiosity, and shared purpose. These guidelines help ensure our spaces remain welcoming and productive.

---

## 💬 Communication Principles

- Assume good intent  
- Ask questions instead of making assumptions  
- Be clear and concise  
- Avoid sarcasm or hostile tone  
- Celebrate wins — big or small  

---

## 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Collaboration Expectations

- Respect others’ time and expertise  
- Provide constructive feedback  
- Keep discussions focused on the work  
- Share knowledge generously  
- Document decisions for future contributors  

---

## 🧵 Issue & PR Etiquette

### When opening an issue:

- Search existing issues first  
- Provide context and reproduction steps  
- Use labels when possible  

### When submitting a PR:

- Keep it focused  
- Write a clear description  
- Include tests when appropriate  
- Be open to feedback  

### When reviewing:

- Be kind  
- Be specific  
- Suggest improvements, not demands  
- Acknowledge good work  

---

## 🧘 Healthy Community Culture

We encourage:

- Learning in public  
- Asking for help  
- Pair programming  
- Sharing resources  
- Mentoring new contributors  

We discourage:

- Gatekeeping  
- Dismissing newcomers  
- Unconstructive criticism  
- Overly negative or combative behavior  

---

## 🚫 Zero Tolerance

The following are not allowed:

- Harassment  
- Hate speech  
- Discrimination  
- Personal attacks  
- Doxxing  
- Spam  

Violations may result in removal from the community.

---

## 🌟 Our Goal

To build a community where people feel safe, supported, and inspired to contribute.
